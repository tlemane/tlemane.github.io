Téo Lemane
teo.lemane@genoscope.cns.fr


I am a postdoctoral researcher in the LAGE team at Genoscope. My research work focuses on algorithms and data structures for large-scale processing of complex sequencing banks as metagenomes from the Tara projet. Previously I have developed methods and tools for constructing, streaming and filtering k-mers matrices from large sequencing collections.

Software

  • kmtricks: kmtricks is a tool suite for counting k-mers, and constructing bloom filters or counted kmer matrices from large and numerous read sets.
  • kmdiff: kmdiff is a tool allowing differential k-mers analysis on large cohorts.
  • kmindex: kmindex is a tool for querying presence/absence k-mer matrices computed by kmtricks.
